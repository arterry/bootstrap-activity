Short Bio

Born as Annie Erin Clark in Tulsa, Oklahoma, St. Vicnent played in many bands before her solo debut as an artist in 2003. Her first solo album was released in 2007 and has seen a rise in popularity ever since.

Genre

Most of her work revolves around a band setup with her playing the lead guitar. She is known to make certain pop sounds, however she changes her style of playing depending on the album. On certain occasions she plays other instruments like the piano, showing her level of expertise as a musician.

Latest Album

Her latest album titled 'Daddy's Home' shows a range of styles and influences that makes her music unique and recognizable. She was influenced by a lot of older 70's synthesizer music and 60's sunflower music. This style was intentional and seperates her from other artists at the moment.

Future

With an album borrowing from 60's and 70's music there is no concrete dirrection on where her style of music will go from here. At the moment many artists are borrowing from 90's music and aethetics, with St. Vincent however there is a bit of suspense on what her next move will be.
